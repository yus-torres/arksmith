What We Do

Our Process

Consultation

We will work closely with you and your qualified representatives to ensure that the services or furniture pieces you ordered perfectly match your design and functional requirements.

Execution

We will start producing the furniture pieces upon your approval of the final designs and specifications.

Inspection

We will pre-schedule shop inspections to be attended by you or your qualified representative so you can check the materials and processes we use in manufacturing.

Delivery

We will deliver your furniture pieces on time and with utmost care.

After Sales

We will provide a notarized warranty certificate for your furniture. We will also turnover a furniture care manual that will serve as your guide in maintaining the furniture pieces.
